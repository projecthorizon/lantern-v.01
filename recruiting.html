Recruiting Approach

We approach recruiting through a different lens. We believe that the cost of a poorly placed candidate has exponentially detrimental effects a team, candidate, and the bottom line. Conversely, a “good fit” placement has exponential effects to the positive. While this isn’t new insight, our experience shows that most recruiters don’t spend the necessary time and effort to maximize “right fit” placement. We do.

We work in collaboration with our clients to analyze the company culture, values, needs and team dynamics in order to first maximize their existing talent before we start bringing new candidates to the table. It has been our experience that identifying and thoroughly understanding a company’s needs and deficiencies leads to the greatest chance for success. We have found that clear, transparent communication is critical for success. To that aim, we carefully design all our engagements to ensure timelines, deliverables and expectations are clearly communicated and understood. Each engagement is a collaboration and partnership, we aim to operate as a natural extension of your core team with your long term success as our primary focus.

It is in our best interest to commit to your long term success. Building and maintaining a deep relationship focused on your success is the best way to achieve our goals. We do this by:

  • Fostering trust and building lifelong relationships with our clients
  • Sharing data, marketplace trends and expertise
  • Customizing scope, strategy and resourcing for each project
  • Embracing our client’s vision, values, culture and pain points
  • Leveraging best practices developed across multiple disciplines to ensure the right hire
  • Making sure that placement isn’t the success metric, a happily productive team with the new hire long after placement is our measure of success
